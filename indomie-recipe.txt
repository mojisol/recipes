- 2 indomie noodles
- 1 carrot
- cabbgage
- 1 egg
- tomatoe sauce
- 1 raw tomatoe
- Onion
 
Directions
1. Boil water, then when boiling pour the tomatoe sauce, then add your two indomie noodles.
2. When indomie is very soft, add the seasoning, then slice the raw tomatoe and onion.
3. When the indomie is done, Cook the raw egg separately, then dish it out.
4. Wash the Cabbage well, and slice some on the prepared dish
5. Your delicious indomie is ready...
